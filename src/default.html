Currency Integrator for CRM Invoices

Currency Integrator for CRM Invoices is a powerful Chrome extension designed to streamline the invoicing process for businesses dealing with international currencies. Leveraging real-time data from the Polish National Bank (NBP), this extension ensures that your CRM invoicing reflects the most current and accurate currency exchange rates.

Features:

  • Real-Time Currency Rates: Fetches the latest exchange rates from the Polish National Bank's official API, providing up-to-the-minute accuracy.
  • Seamless Integration with CRM: Automatically populates the exchange rates into your CRM Invoice Page, eliminating manual entry and minimizing errors.
  • One-Click Conversion: Enables quick conversion between PLN and foreign currencies right within the CRM Invoice Page with a simple click.
  • Secure and Efficient: Operates securely within the Chrome browser, maintaining user privacy and ensuring rapid performance.

How to Use:

  1. Installation: Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Manual Refresh: Use the "fetch button" below the "Foreign currency to PLN exchange rate" text box to manually refresh rates or access additional options.

Permissions Required:

  • Access to CRM: To integrate with your CRM Invoice Page.
  • Internet Access: To fetch the latest currency exchange rates from the Polish National Bank.
